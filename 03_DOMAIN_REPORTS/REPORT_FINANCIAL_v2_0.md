## §19. Glossary

**Ashtakavarga / Sarvashtakavarga (SAV):** A classical bindu-scoring system where each planet contributes benefic or malefic bindus to each house based on its natal position. SAV = sum of all 8 planets' contributions. A house with SAV < 25 is structurally weak for delivery; > 30 is strong.

**Amatya Karaka (AmK):** In Jaimini astrology, the planet with the second-highest longitude in the natal chart — the career-significator. Saturn is the native's AmK.

**Atmakaraka (AK):** In Jaimini astrology, the planet with the highest longitude in the natal chart — the soul-significator. Moon is the native's AK.

**Bhavabala:** Classical house-strength metric computed as the sum of several sub-components (Bhava Adhipati Bala, Bhava Dig Bala, Bhava Drishti Bala, Bhava Drekkana). Higher Bhavabala = structurally stronger house.

**Bhrigu Bindu (BB):** A sensitive point computed as (Moon + Rahu) / 2. The natal BB is at Libra 8°04' (7H). Progresses approximately 0.8° per year. At age 42 (2026), BB has progressed to Gemini 3H (UL's sign).

**Chalit:** The degree-based house chart (Sripathi system) used for assigning planets to houses. A planet within 5-15° of a house cusp may "fall into" the previous house in the Chalit system even if its Rashi (sign) placement is in the next house.

**Chara Karaka:** Jaimini's moving significator system where each planet holds a karaka role based on its longitude rank. The roles change from chart to chart.

**Dasha Activation Score (DAS):** A composite score combining eight activation factors — normalized to 0-100. DAS 75-89 = Peak tier; 90-100 = Critical tier; 60-74 = Elevated.

**Guru Chandal Yoga:** Jupiter conjunct Rahu — produces unconventional, boundary-crossing expression of Jupiter's dharmic signification. In D9 (native's Gemini 12H), applies to the Maha Dhana terminal dispositor's dharma-chart expression.

**Karakamsa:** The D9 house containing the Atmakaraka — the soul-purpose chart's operative domain. Native's Karakamsa is Gemini D9 12H.

**Maha Dhana Yoga:** A high-order Dhana Yoga formed when all five wealth-house lords (1L, 2L, 5L, 9L, 11L) are connected in a closed circuit. Native's chart: confirmed through double derivation. CYSS 83.

**Muntha:** Tajika (annual chart) focal point, progressing one sign per year from the natal Lagna. For 2026-27: Libra 7H.

**NBRY (Neecha Bhanga Raja Yoga):** A yoga formed when a debilitated planet's debilitation is cancelled. Produces delayed-but-high-peak delivery in the planet's domains.

**Pratyantardasha (PD):** Sub-sub-period within an Antardasha in the Vimshottari system. Approximately 2-3 months duration.

**Saraswati Yoga:** A wealth-knowledge yoga formed when Mercury, Jupiter, and Venus occupy kendra or trikona houses with favorable dignities. CYSS 91 in native's chart.

**Shadbala:** The classical sixfold planetary strength metric, measured in Rupas. Classical minimum thresholds range from 5.00 (Moon, Mercury, Venus) to 6.50 (Sun, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn).

**Shree Lagna:** A Tajika sensitive point indicating prosperity — native's Shree Lagna at Sagittarius 24°15' (co-locates with Lakshmi-Maha-Dhana seat).

**Shuddha Pinda:** Benefic bindu-support from the Ashtakavarga system, measuring cumulative karmic "permission" for a planet's deliveries.

**Uccha Bala:** A sub-component of Shadbala measuring proximity to exaltation degree. Maximum (60 virupa) when exactly at exaltation degree; minimum at exact debilitation degree.

**Upapada Lagna (UL):** The Jaimini Arudha for the 12H — reflects the external manifestation of the marriage/partnership dimension. Native's UL is in Gemini 3H.

**Varshphal:** The Tajika annual chart computed from the solar return. Year Lord for 2026-27: Jupiter.

**Vimshottari Mahadasha / Antardasha:** The 120-year Parashari dasha system. Native's current position: Mercury MD (2010-2027) / Saturn AD (2024-2027).

**Vimshottari Dasha sub-periods:** MD (Mahadasha, major period) → AD (Antardasha, sub-period, 1-3 years) → PD (Pratyantardasha, sub-sub-period, 2-3 months) → SP (Sookshma, 2-3 weeks) → PP (Prana, 1-2 days).

**Vimsopaka Bala:** Planetary strength across the 16 divisional charts, normalized to 20 points. Native's Jupiter: rank 1 (12.1/20) — dispersed-but-stable multi-varga strength.

**Vimshottari Yogini:** A separate 36-year dasha system. Native's current: Bhadrika (Mercury) — through December 22, 2026; then Ulka (Saturn) — 2026-2032.
