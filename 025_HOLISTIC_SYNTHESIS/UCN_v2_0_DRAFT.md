The Mercury Pentagram describes the five life-domains Mercury simultaneously governs — this is an operational consequence worthy of UCN-level articulation.

## §III — NEW CONCEPT: COMPENSATED-EMPTINESS PRINCIPLE (promote from Children §4)

### Concept

Some empty houses in this chart are compensated by strong aspect-coverage or lord-strength, while others are not. The 5H empty-but-Jupiter-aspected is a compensated emptiness: Jupiter's 9th special aspect from 9H own-sign reaches 5H directly. Empty but architecturally supported.

**Contrast**: 4H Cancer empty + Mandi shadow + Moon-lord-displaced-to-11H = uncompensated-emptiness (house lacks both tenants AND supportive aspects except Saturn 10th-aspect — itself ambiguous).

### Why it belongs in UCN v2.0

This is a meta-principle for reading empty houses across any chart, generalized from this specific native's architecture. It deserves UCN-level formalization as a reading convention.

### Draft UCN §XIII text

> Empty houses in this chart should be read through the Compensated-Emptiness principle: empty-AND-supported ≠ empty-AND-unsupported. Support comes via (a) own-sign-lord placement in a strong house, (b) aspect from exalted/own-sign benefic, (c) co-location of supportive Arudha/Saham. 5H exemplifies (b): Jupiter own-sign 9H → 9th special aspect → 5H. 4H exemplifies (c weak): Moon-lord-displaced + Mandi present. 12H exemplifies (a): Jupiter 12L in own sign 9H. Reading empty houses without applying this principle over-pessimizes. Applying it produces proportionate-to-architecture readings.
