## §2 — DECISION-SUPPORT TEMPLATES

### §2.1 — Major Career Move (new role, new company, new venture)

**Chart checks**:
1. Does this role deploy Mercury at its seven-system-designed capacity? (analytical, advisory, intellectual bridge-building)
2. Does this role pass the wealth-as-dharmic-output test? (dharmic alignment structurally prerequisite)
3. Does the timing fit the current dasha phase? (Mercury MD institutionalization through 2027; Ketu MD transformation 2031+)
4. Does this role expand or compromise the 11H AK soul-community orientation? (purposeful co-creators required)
5. Does this preserve or disrupt the 12H foreign-residence architectural endorsement?

**Red flags**:
- Role requires non-dharmic commercial activity (bypassing the 9H authorization channel)
- Role disrupts Singapore base without replacing the four-mechanism foreign architecture
- Role over-extends Mercury 6L (cognitive overload pattern)
- Role removes Saturn-structural integration (e.g., pure Mars-reactive environments)

### §2.2 — Major Relationship Decision (commitment, separation, redefinition)

**Chart checks**:
1. Is the decision in alignment with the BB-UL 2026 crystallization window intention? (2026 favors clarity, not impulsive action)
2. Does the relationship pass the Saturn-structural test? (long-term durability, genuine commitment frame)
3. Does it allow ATT to function productively? (productive tension, not suppressed-or-destructive)
4. Does the partner align with the A7-AK 11H soul-community criterion?
5. Does the partner fit the Mercury-UL quality (intellectual-communicative, multi-register)?

**Red flags**:
- Relationship seeks to eliminate the ATT (conventional-harmony preference conflicts with chart architecture)
- Partner does not engage Mercury at depth (Mercury-UL mismatch)
- Decision made in reactive Mars-urgency rather than Saturn-structural deliberation

### §2.3 — Major Financial Commitment (investment, purchase, large expenditure)

**Chart checks**:
1. Does the investment align with dharmic domain (education, advisory, intellectual work, devotional)?
2. Does it honor Rahu-2H patient accumulation vs. Ketu-8H release-seeking tension?
3. Does the timing fit the current Varsha and dasha period?
4. Is the capital source dharmically-aligned wealth?
5. Does the commitment disrupt or preserve the Mercury Pentagram's integrated functioning?

**Red flags**:
- Speculative high-risk investment (Rahu-2H learning frontier prefers patient, not speculative)
- Commitment exceeds sustainable cash flow (forces Mercury into 6L over-deployment to compensate)
- Lifestyle expansion without dharmic grounding (wealth-as-dharmic-output principle violation)

### §2.4 — Major Health Intervention (medical procedure, new regimen)

**Chart checks**:
1. What does the 36-month heatmap say about the current health-window? (Nov 2026-Mar 2027 = high-risk; avoid elective non-urgent procedures)
2. Does the intervention align with Pitta-Vata constitution?
3. Does it address Vata nervous system sensitivity specifically?

- Intensive Pitta-aggravating interventions (highly spicy, alcohol-intensive, high-inflammation)
- Unmanaged Vata-aggravating schedules (irregular eating, sleep disruption, travel intensity)

## §3 — DECISION-MAKING PROTOCOL

For each major decision:

1. **Classify** the decision type (§2.1-2.7)
2. **Apply chart checks** for that type
3. **Flag red flags** if any
4. **Route through Whole-Chart-Read Protocol** (Query Prompt Library)
5. **Consider current timing window** (Heatmap + dasha)
6. **Check against Operating Instructions** (UCN §X five OIs)
7. **Make decision with clarity about trade-offs**
8. **Log decision + outcome** for future retrodictive testing

## §4 — THE FIVE OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS (UCN §X) AS DECISION FILTERS

Every major decision should be tested against UCN §X:

- **OI1 — Deploy Mercury Without Apology**: Is Mercury fully deployed or constrained?
- **OI2 — Treat Pressure as Resource**: Is the decision embracing or avoiding ATT pressure?
- **OI3 — Align with Dharma Before Optimizing Returns**: Is dharmic alignment the starting point?
- **OI4 — Tend 7H as Primary Inner Practice**: Does the decision maintain 7H-tending discipline?
- **OI5 — Build to Offer Not to Own**: Is the decision oriented toward offering (12H from Moon) or accumulation?

A decision that passes all five OIs is chart-aligned. A decision that violates one or more deserves re-examination.
