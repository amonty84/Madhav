Remedial practice in this chart follows three principles derived from the L2.5 synthesis:

1. **Strengthen what is already architecturally strong, do not compensate what is weak.** The chart's architecture has pre-decided which planetary energies carry its curriculum. Remedial practice that amplifies Mercury (the chart's primary instrument, 7-system designated) and Jupiter (dharma lord own-sign) produces disproportionate return. Remedial practice that tries to "fix" architecturally-designed tensions (Saturn-Mars 7H ATT, Venus 9H dharmic-not-personal expression) counteracts the chart's design.

Heavy elaborate ritual-compilation practice does not match the chart's natural register.

3. **Don't remediate enemies; befriend them.** Saturn (AmK, exalted 7H) is not an afflicting planet in this chart — it is the chart's organizing principle. Mars (Avayogi, 7H, Lagna lord) is not adversarial — it is the drive-architecture. Ketu (exalted 8H) is not malefic here — it is the moksha-karaka in its highest dignity. The classical "Saturn remediation" or "Mars pacification" protocols misread this chart; they would diminish the chart's authentic authority.
