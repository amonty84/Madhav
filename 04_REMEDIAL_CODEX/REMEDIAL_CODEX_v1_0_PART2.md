## §1 — YANTRA RECOMMENDATIONS

Yantras are geometric-diagrammatic meditative tools. Chart-aligned yantras for this native:

### §1.1 — Primary: Shree Yantra

**Chart rationale**: Shree Lagna in 9H + Venus (Ishta Devata Mahalakshmi) 9H + Lakshmi-Narayana-adjacent configuration = Shree Yantra is the most architecturally-aligned yantra. It is the geometric representation of Shree (Lakshmi-dharma) — the same principle as the Shree Lagna in 9H.

**Installation**:
- Silver-plated or gold-plated (preferred) — Venus's metal aligns
- Size: Tabletop 4-6 inch; larger for pooja room
- Placement: North-east corner of home, or personal dharmic-practice space
- Activation: Shree Yantra-specific prana pratishtha by a qualified acharya before first use
- Daily practice: Simple daily gaze/acknowledgment (even 2-3 minutes) + weekly Shree Sukta recitation

### §1.2 — Secondary: Sudarshana Yantra (Vishnu's discus)

**Chart rationale**: Vishnu/Venkateshwara = Ishta/Dharma Devata. Sudarshana is Vishnu's primary yantra, representing dharmic protection.

**Installation**: In home or office as protection-yantra. Weekly Sudarshana Ashtaka-recitation supports.

### §1.3 — Optional: Saraswati Yantra

**Chart rationale**: Mercury is the chart's primary instrument. Saraswati Yantra (Saraswati is the Vedic goddess of wisdom/learning associated with Mercury's expression) is architecturally-aligned for intellectual-dharmic practice.

**Installation**: In study space. Daily acknowledgment before analytical work blocks.

### §1.4 — NOT recommended: Mangal Yantra, Shani Yantra, Rahu/Ketu Yantras

Consistent with the Part 1 principle: the "malefic pacification" yantras misread this chart. Saturn and Mars are integral to the chart's architecture; pacifying them diminishes the chart's authentic authority.

## §2 — TEMPLE/DEVATA-SPECIFIC PRACTICES

### §2.1 — Tirumala Venkateshwara Connection

**Chart rationale**: Triple-lock Vishnu alignment (Palana Devata Mercury-Vishnu; Dharma Devata Saturn-Venkateshwara; Ishta Devata Venus-Mahalakshmi with Lakshmi as Vishnu's consort).

**Practices**:
- **Annual pilgrimage** (if feasible): Tirumala visit during Jupiter or Mercury transits supports alignment
- **Venkateshwara Suprabhatam**: Morning chant (listening or recitation). 4 AM audio-track from Tirumala Devasthanam.
- **Donations to TTD (Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanam)**: Temple-service donations align with both Venkateshwara devotion and dharmic Mercury-activation.
- **Home shrine with Venkateshwara image/small vigraha**: If household practice is desired, Venkateshwara shrine is the chart-most-aligned

### §2.2 — Vishnu Temple Practices

**Any Vishnu form** aligns: Venkateshwara (primary), Ranganatha (Srirangam), Padmanabhaswamy (Thiruvananthapuram), Jagannath (Puri — note: native's Bhubaneswar home region).

**Jagannath consideration**: The native is from Bhubaneswar, and Jagannath is a Vishnu form native to Odisha. However, the chart's Dharma Devata is specifically Venkateshwara (derived from Saturn's Aquarius = Dharma Devata sign-lord). If the native has a preexisting Jagannath connection (family/regional), this remains aligned (Jagannath is also a Vishnu form), but Venkateshwara has the tightest chart-specific activation. CTR.02 in Deep Analysis v1.2.1 noted tension between the operational Jagannath-regional-identity and the Dharma-Devata Venkateshwara; the 2025 spiritual pivot empirically resolved this in favor of Venkateshwara.

### §2.3 — Temple Visit Timing

**Optimal planetary transits for Vishnu-temple engagement**:
- Jupiter transit through Sagittarius (9H) or Pisces (12H)
- Mercury transit through Gemini (3H UL house) or Virgo (6H) or Capricorn (10H) — Mercury in its rulerships
- Sun transit through Sagittarius (9H) — annual window approximately mid-Dec to mid-Jan
- Full Moon transits when Moon transits Cancer (4H) or through own Purva-Bhadrapada

**Avoid for new major spiritual commitments**:
- Amavasya (new moon) is neutral — not auspicious nor forbidden
- Mars/Saturn retrograde transits — not forbidden but less optimally-activated
- Eclipse days — consult specific panchang guidance
